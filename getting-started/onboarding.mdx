Welcome to Datum. This page walks you through your first steps as a new team member.

## Before Day One

- [ ] Accept your offer letter and complete paperwork
- [ ] Set up your work email
- [ ] Get access to GitHub, Slack, and Notion (see [Tools](/operations/tools))
- [ ] Review the [Company Overview](/company/about)

## Week One

- [ ] Meet your manager and team
- [ ] Complete [Day One checklist](/getting-started/day-one)
- [ ] Read the [Values](/company/values)
- [ ] Review [Benefits](/people/benefits) and enrollment deadlines

## Key Contacts

Reach out to the People team for any onboarding questions. See the [Team Roster](/people/team) for contacts.
