## Morning

- [ ] Set up your laptop (see IT setup guide in Slack `#it-help`)
- [ ] Log into your Datum Google account
- [ ] Join Slack and introduce yourself in `#general`
- [ ] Add yourself to the [Team Roster](/people/team)

## Accounts & Access

- [ ] GitHub — request access to [datum-cloud](https://github.com/datum-cloud)
- [ ] Verify you can access the internal wiki
- [ ] Set up 2FA on all accounts

## Admin

- [ ] Review your offer letter and confirm start details
- [ ] Enroll in [Benefits](/people/benefits) (deadline: 30 days from start)
- [ ] Familiarize yourself with [PTO policy](/people/pto)

## Get to Know the Team

- [ ] Schedule 1:1s with your direct team
- [ ] Read [Company Overview](/company/about) and [Values](/company/values)

<Note>
  Questions? Ask in `#people` on Slack.
</Note>
