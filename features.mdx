We are currently building a public cloud with a generous "forever free" tier, and working closely with design partners on managed cloud and BYOC approaches.

## Current Capabilities

Our public cloud is opinionated, but powered by key partners. We call these our "house brand" services:

- **AI Edge** - An envoy edge proxy and AI gateway, powered by Tetrate
- **Connectors** - A way to orchestrate connections, starting with QUIC tunnels (powered by Iroh) but planned to include other developer (e.g. Tailscale Tailnets, Wireguard VPNs) or telco (AWS Direct Connect, Equinix Fabric, Megaport Onramps, etc) connections. 
- **Essentials**: DNS, domains, OTel metrics export, activity logs, collaboration, etc.

## Roadmap

We're currently working on the following major features:

- **UFOs** - Millisecond cold-start, isolated compute that scales to zero, powered by Unikraft. UFO stands for Unikernel Function Offload, but really it's just a fun name for a futurist "workers" feature. 
- **Galactic VPC** - Virtual private backbones that span anywhere, powered by SRv6
- **vMMR** - A virtual "meet me room" for interacting with diverse connections

The best place to track our roadmap is via our [website or milestones](https://www.datum.net/roadmap/). To add or suggest features, please visit our [GitHub discussions](https://github.com/orgs/datum-cloud/discussions/categories/feature-requests) page!
