## Who We Are

- **Connectors** — of people, businesses, apps, and networks
- **Operators at heart** — we build and run things, we don't just talk about them
- **Passionate about working in the open** — transparency is a core part of how we operate

## How We Act

- Find creative solutions to complex problems
- Prioritize outcomes for users, customers, and partners
- Write our own words — maintain an authentic voice
- Assume positive intent — start from a place of trust in every conversation
- Celebrate impact and outcomes over hours worked

## How We Work

Datum is **remote-first** and **async-first**. We are a globally distributed team and we've designed our culture and tooling around that.

### Remote-First

We default to written communication and documented decisions over instant responses. Every important decision should be findable later.

### Async-First

Respect deep focus and personal time. Avoid overusing @channel notifications. Mix scheduled syncs with ad-hoc catchups, and recognize the value of different communication modalities.

### Open by Default

Our strategy is centered around transparency. Before making something private, identify a specific reason it shouldn't be public. Exceptions exist for customer privacy, detailed financials, or unannounced partnerships.

Strategic and product conversations happen in public GitHub Discussions to include team members, community participants, and customers in shaping our direction.

### Autonomy with Alignment

We give team members clear goals and context, then get out of the way. Work openly in public channels and GitHub issues. Own your work — disagree respectfully, but commit once a decision is made.
