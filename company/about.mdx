Datum is a venture-backed startup reimagining the internet for the AI era. We're building an open-source network cloud that gives developers access to internet infrastructure capabilities that have historically been reserved for the largest cloud providers — global edges, private networks, deterministic routing, and direct interconnection.

**Legal name:** Datum Technology, Inc.
**Incorporated:** 2023 (active development from late 2024)
**HQ:** New York City, with a globally distributed team

## Mission

Help the next 1,000 clouds thrive in the AI era.

Our 10-year vision: *"The most trusted way for AI & digital ecosystems to interact — with millions of active connections to prove it."*

## The Problem We're Solving

Distributed workloads, complex regulations, and security concerns leave developers working with at least one hand tied behind their backs. The capabilities that large enterprises leverage easily — global edges, private backbones, cloud onramps, peering — have been out of reach for the builders who need them most.

Datum fixes that by providing internet superpowers in the tools builders already use, like Cursor and Kubernetes.

## Open Source

Our core platform is AGPLv3 open source. This is foundational to our strategy — it builds trust, eliminates lock-in, enables community contributions, and reinforces our position as a neutral operator.
