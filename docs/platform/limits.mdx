---
title: "Quotas"
---

Datum is in active development and currently only offers a free tier with reasonable limits on resources. Resource limits (e.g. quotas) can be found under the setting areas for Organizations and Projects. 

<Frame caption="Example quotas for a personal project">
  ![Screenshot2026 03 23at9 55 42PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-23at9.55.42PM.png)
</Frame>

If you run into quotas that constrain your work, simply drop a note to [support@datum.net](mailto:support@datum.net) or the live chat widget accessible at the top of the portal. 

[Discord](https://link.datum.net/discord) is also an option!
