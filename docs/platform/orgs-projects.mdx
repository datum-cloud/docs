We use a standard cloud data model that associated Projects within Organizations. There are two types of Orgs:

- **Personal Org**- An entity created for each user that does not support collaboration and cannot be renamed. Consider this your personal sandbox.
- **Standard Orgs** - Ability to collaborate on projects, share resources, and centralize management.

## Team Management

To collaborate with team members, you’ll need to create or join a Standard Organization. Standard Organizations support the following coarse-grained roles:

- **Owners** - Full control over the organization and all resources.
- **Editors** - Control over all resources in an organization, except for team members.
- **Viewer** - Read only access to all resources in an organization.
