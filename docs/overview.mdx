Datum is an 'open network cloud' designed to unlock internet superpowers for every agent, app, and builder. We provide network and connectivity services but targeted at 100% programmatic and agentic use cases.

Unlike a traditional CDN or hyperscaler cloud:

- We are 100% focused on modern, "alt cloud" service providers
- Our code is open source (AGPLv3) so you can run it anywhere if needed. 

Our product is evolving quickly and we currently only offer a free tier. We make money by providing infrastructure and network aggregation via our public cloud, and by managing private or custom edge clouds.

## Background

Founded in late 2024, we're built by a team of infrastructure veterans and investors from Amplify, Cervin, CRV, Encoded, ex/ante, Step Function, and Vine Ventures. Previously we've helped to build infrastructure companies like Packet, Voxel, SoftLayer, StackPath, Highwinds, Zscaler, and many more.

## Context

Our platform provides traditional internet-scale primitives (e.g. intelligent edge proxies, private backbones, cloud onramps, peering, meet-me-rooms, etc) but with an experience that is 100% programmatic, agent-first, and backed by AGPLv3 open source.

We believe that a network cloud in the AI era is a bit different than a traditional CDN, mainly because the use case is intelligence not caching. Additionally, we think that open (open source \+ open ecosystems) is a critical requirement in a world that is increasingly fragmented and regulated.

Our target customer is a modern service provider, which we lovingly call [Alt Clouds](https://www.alt-cloud.org/). We aim to support 1k's of them, so neutrality and transparency are important features in everything we do.

## Our Trends

We started Datum because we saw three big trends:

1. Intelligence & Data Everywhere
2. 1000's of New Providers
3. An Era of Increased Regulation
