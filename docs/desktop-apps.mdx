Datum offers native apps for macOS, Linux and Windows with the current goal of helping you to quickly and safely expose local environments to the internet. Download and installation instructions [can be found here](https://www.datum.net/download/).

To expose a local environment to the internet, Datum creates a secure, Rust-based tunnel (built on the Iroh protocol) and connects it to a fresh AI Edge.
