## Engineering & Planning

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|

## Internal Software

**Milo** — Datum's proprietary internal platform for operating the cloud service. It serves as the system of record, staff portal, and core workflows around users and customers.

## Philosophy

We take an **organized chaos approach** to tooling — team members are encouraged to use the tools that suit them best. What matters is that work is visible and decisions are documented.

## Getting Access

New to the team? See [Tools & Access](/getting-started/tools) for how to get set up. For anything not listed, ask in `#ops` on Slack.

## Requesting New Software

1. Post in `#ops` on Slack with the tool name, cost, and use case
2. Get manager approval
3. Operations handles procurement and licensing
