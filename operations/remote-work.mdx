Datum is a **remote-first, async-first** company with a globally distributed team. We've designed our culture and tooling around this from day one.

## Expectations

- Default to **written communication** — document decisions so they're findable later
- Keep your **Slack status** up to date, especially when focused or offline
- Add your **time zone** to your Slack profile and calendar
- Attend required syncs regardless of location
- Respect deep focus and personal time — avoid overusing @channel notifications

## Core Hours

We work across time zones. When scheduling syncs, be mindful of overlap. For async matters, don't expect an instant response outside someone's working hours.

## Communication

- **Slack** — internal async communication
- **GitHub Discussions** — open cross-team and community conversations  
- **Scheduled syncs** — mixed with ad-hoc catchups as needed

The goal is visibility without constant interruption. Work openly in public channels where possible.

## Home Office

New hires receive a setup stipend. See [Benefits](/people/benefits) for details.

## Travel & Offsites

The team gathers periodically. Travel logistics and expenses are covered by Datum. See [Expenses](/operations/expenses) for the reimbursement process.
